About Me

I am a UI/UX designer with over 3 years of experience in user experience design. I worked at Banco Pichincha, the largest bank in Ecuador, where I was involved in significant projects for an audience of over 4 million people. I thoroughly enjoy creative work, learning new things, and finding innovative solutions through design and user experience. I have a strong knowledge of design software, including Figma, prototyping tools and the Adobe stack (Illustrator, Photoshop, After Effects, Audition, Premiere Pro). Additionally, I have knowledge in UX research techniques, user interviews, and usability testing. I also have knowledge in computing and programming, which enhances my ability to collaborate effectively with development teams.
